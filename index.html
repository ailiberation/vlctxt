Color Shift Keying (CSK) Modulation

In this document, we explore the use of Color Shift Keying (CSK) modulation, highlighting its advantages over On-Off Keying (OOK) in terms of noise immunity and ambient light resistance.

Benefits of CSK

  • Improved Noise Immunity: CSK modulation is more robust against interference, making it suitable for environments with high noise levels.
  • Ambient Light Resistance: Unlike OOK, CSK is less affected by varying light conditions, ensuring reliable performance.

We recommend implementing CSK for applications requiring higher reliability under challenging conditions. This document serves as a foundational understanding of CSK and its applications.
